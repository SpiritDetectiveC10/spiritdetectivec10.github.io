Nyan Cat

Nyan Cat is an animated cartoon cat with a Pop-Tart body that leaves trail rainbow trail when flying. It originated from a youtube video posted in 2011 which combined the animation with a Japanese pop song by Hatsune Miku. The character was created by Christian Torres via a GIF animation and Youtuber saraj00n combined the GIF with the song Nyanyan by Hatsune Miku and uploaded it to Youtube shortly after. Nyan Cat's original name was simply "Pop Tart Cat" but was dubbed "Nyan Cat" named after the song. The creator later accepted the name change due its catchiness.

Pictures

Nyan Cat is widely regarded as one of the most viral memes from the 2010s. The video has about 6.8 million videos and has gained a large following with several video games being created from its popularity. In 2021, Nyan Cat became an NFT and sold for about $515,817.
